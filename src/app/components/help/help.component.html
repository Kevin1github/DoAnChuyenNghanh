Help Center

How to log in:

  1. Go to the Login page using the navigation menu
  2. Enter your username and password
  3. Click the Login button

For demonstration purposes:

  • Admin access: username admin, password admin
  • User access: username user, password user

If you can't log in: Contact system administrator for assistance.

How to pay a bill:

  1. Log in with your user account
  2. Navigate to "Pay Bills" in the menu
  3. Find the bill you want to pay in the table
  4. Click the "Pay Now" button next to the bill
  5. Confirm the payment details
  6. Complete the payment process

Payment confirmation: After successful payment, the bill status will change to "Paid" and you'll receive a confirmation.

How to update your profile:

  1. Log in with your user account
  2. Navigate to "My Profile" in the menu
  3. Update your information in the form fields
  4. Click the "Update Profile" button to save changes

Note: Keeping your profile information up-to-date ensures you receive important notifications about your account and bills.
